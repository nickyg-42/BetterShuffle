Spotify Shuffler

Log In With Spotify To Use      

How do I use it?

  • 1. Log in with Spotify
  • 2. Select a device
  • 3. Click shuffle on the desired playlist

Note: Once a playlist is shuffled you can close this page or visit it again to re-shuffle.

How does it work?

It gets all the tracks from the playlist you selected and then adds them to your queue in a randomized order.

If it is not working:

The app has probably exceeded its hourly request limit. Try again in an hour.
